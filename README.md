1. Abstract
SikaToken (SIKA) is a BEP-20 digital asset built on the BNB Smart Chain, designed to enable scalable user onboarding through a hybrid model combining off-chain engagement and on-chain token distribution.
The protocol integrates fair launch tokenomics, deep liquidity provisioning, AI-powered automation, gaming incentives, staking, governance, and deflationary mechanisms to create a sustainable and growth-oriented ecosystem.
SikaToken focuses on real user adoption, controlled supply distribution, and long-term value creation, avoiding common pitfalls such as whale dominance and unsustainable inflation.

6. Tokenomic Strategy
Fair Launch (40%)
A large public allocation ensures decentralized ownership, prevents whale concentration, and builds immediate trust within the community.
Deep Liquidity (30%)
Allocating a significant portion to liquidity ensures stable trading conditions, minimizes slippage, and protects against extreme volatility.
Staking Incentives (15%)
Encourages long-term holding, reduces sell pressure, and creates a sustainable reward loop within the ecosystem.
Lean Team Allocation (3%)
A reduced and fully vested team allocation signals long-term commitment and minimizes rug-pull concerns.
Targeted Airdrops (5%)
Instead of mass distribution, SikaToken focuses on high-quality users to maximize engagement and reduce bot activity.

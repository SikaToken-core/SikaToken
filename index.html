SikaToken Whitepaper

Digital Wealth Built on BNB Smart Chain

1. Introduction

SikaToken is a community-driven digital asset built on the BNB Smart Chain. It focuses on onboarding new users into the crypto ecosystem through incentives, education, and structured token distribution.

2. Problem

Many users are unable to access or understand cryptocurrency due to lack of education, high entry barriers, and limited incentives. Most projects fail to build strong communities before launch, leading to poor adoption and rapid token dumping.

3. Solution

SikaToken solves this by implementing a structured airdrop and vesting system that rewards early users while controlling token supply. The project builds a strong community before liquidity launch, ensuring sustainable growth and engagement.

SikaToken (SIKA)

AI • Gaming • DeFi Utility Token on BNB Smart Chain

1. Abstract

SikaToken (SIKA) is a BEP-20 digital asset deployed on the BNB Smart Chain, designed to drive scalable user onboarding through a hybrid model combining off-chain engagement and on-chain token distribution. The protocol integrates AI-powered automation, gaming incentives, staking, governance, and deflationary supply control.

5. Tokenomics

  • 40% — Presale / Fair Launch
    Sold to the public to raise BNB for liquidity and ensure decentralized ownership.
  • 30% — Liquidity Pool (LP)
    Paired with raised BNB and locked for 12+ months to ensure stability.
  • 15% — Staking & Rewards
    Incentivizes long-term holding and liquidity mining participation.
  • 7% — Marketing & Growth
    Used for influencer campaigns, ads, and partnerships.
  • 5% — Airdrop
    Targeted distribution to active BNB/CAKE users to drive viral adoption.
  • 3% — Team (Vested)
    Locked for 6–12 months with gradual monthly release.

6. Tokenomic Strategy

Fair Launch (40%)

A large public allocation prevents whale dominance and builds strong initial trust.

Deep Liquidity (30%)

Ensures sufficient market depth to reduce volatility and protect price stability.

Staking Incentives (15%)

Encourages holding behavior and reduces circulating supply pressure.

Low Team Allocation (3%)

Demonstrates long-term commitment and reduces risk of early sell-offs.

Targeted Airdrops (5%)

Focuses on high-quality users rather than mass farming, improving adoption quality.

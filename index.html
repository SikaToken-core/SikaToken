SikaToken Whitepaper

Technical & Investor-Grade Documentation

1. Abstract

SikaToken (SIKA) is a BEP-20 digital asset deployed on the BNB Smart Chain, designed to drive scalable user onboarding through a hybrid model combining off-chain engagement and on-chain token distribution. The protocol introduces a structured airdrop with vesting, liquidity-controlled market entry, and utility-driven mechanisms including staking, governance, and deflationary supply control.
